Creative Process.

Identify & Clarify

The first step in my process is identifying and defining all aspects of the project. This includes goals, audience, market, competitors, specifications, and requirements. Once I have clear and concise foundational grasp on the desirables, we can begin the process.

Research & Strategize

Once we have established the basics, it is essential to get the background information necessary for the project. This can include user research, survey, interviews, data collection, etc. Compiling all of this information into one coheisve strategy for execution is critical.

Experience Design

This is the where the fun begins. There are a number of tools I like to use while designing the product and ideal user experience which should always be intuitive, visually appealing, and well connected from start to finish.

Refine & Deliver

Once we have the desired product at hand, I like to make sure all parts are functioning as intended. Reiterate. Reiterate. Reiterate so that the final product is exactly what we need. Ideas change and can take on a path of their own, so I want to make sure the final delivery meets and exceeds all expectations.
